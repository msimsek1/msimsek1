THE PRODUCER

Music, Cover Artwork

It is not easy to talk about my producing. It all started with an iPad app. Later my little sister bought me a MIDI Pad. That was the moment where I thought that I need to do something with it. By the help of listening music and especially YouTube i got the feeling of it.

Now I produce beats as a moment to calm down from the everyday life. Mostly i produce all this stuff in the late night. The Art Cover for all the beats are freestyle. I think of something and start creating like my beats. No planning, no concept just freestyle.
Check out my latest beats. Maybe you like one of them.
